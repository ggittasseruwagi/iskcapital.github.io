Curriculum
8 Modules. One Clear Path.

Designed by experienced East African capital markets practitioners — not textbook academics.

01
Capital Markets Overview

What are capital markets? How do the USE and NSE work? Why does Africa need more retail investors?

02
Capital Markets Stakeholders

The full ecosystem — regulators (CMA, USE), brokers, custodians, listed companies, and where you fit in.

03
Market Terminologies

Bid/ask spreads, P/E ratios, yield, dividend yield, market cap — explained in plain East African context.

04
Stocks as a Product

What you're actually buying, how shares are priced, dividends, rights issues, and how to evaluate listed Ugandan and Kenyan companies.

05
Bonds, ETFs, REITs & Derivatives

Beyond equities — the full toolkit for building a diversified African portfolio including government and corporate bonds.

06
Technical & Fundamental Analysis

Reading charts vs. reading balance sheets. We teach both — and how to combine them for better decisions in low-liquidity African markets.

07
Opening Your CSD / CDSC Account

Step-by-step guide to opening your CSD account (USE) or CDSC account (NSE) through an ISK partner broker.

08
Key Investment Tips

Portfolio construction principles, risk management, dollar-cost averaging, and the investor mindset for long-term wealth building in Africa.

About I.S.K Capital

East Africa's Capital Markets
Education Partner

I.S.K Capital was founded in Kampala, Uganda with one mission: to make capital markets participation accessible, understandable, and profitable for every East African — regardless of their starting point.

We operate at the crossroads of financial education and market access. We don't just teach you how markets work; we connect you to the real infrastructure — the brokers, the accounts, and the instruments — needed to participate in Uganda's and Kenya's growing capital markets.

Our analysts bring real-world experience from the USE and NSE, understanding the unique dynamics of frontier and emerging market investing that textbooks from London or New York simply can't teach.

Our Principles
What We Stand For
🎓

Education First

We will never push you into a financial product you don't understand. Our entire model is built on making sure you know exactly what you're buying and why, before you spend a single shilling.

🌍

African Context

Capital markets function differently in frontier economies. Liquidity, currency risk, regulatory frameworks — we teach these realities honestly, not through the lens of developed market assumptions.

🤝

Broker Independence

We partner with multiple brokers — Dyer & Blair, Crested Capital, SBG Securities and others — so we can recommend the right one for your specific needs, not the one that pays us the most.

🔒

Legitimacy & Compliance

We operate within the frameworks of the Capital Markets Authority of Uganda and Capital Markets Authority of Kenya. Our recommendations follow regulated financial advice standards.

💰

Radical Affordability

$15 is our entry point — deliberately. We believe the wealth gap in East Africa is partly an information gap. Access to quality financial education should not itself be a luxury.

📈

Long-Term Thinking

We do not teach get-rich-quick strategies. We teach portfolio construction, risk management, and the disciplined investor mindset that builds real, durable wealth over years and decades.
